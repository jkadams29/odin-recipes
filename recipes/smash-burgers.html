Smash Burgers

Awesome smash burgers!

Smash burgers are the best way to make amazing burgers that will be the envy of your neighborhood!

Throw out the old way of pounding ground beef into patties, and step into the 21st century of backyard grilling!

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Dales sauce
  • Teriyaki sauce
  • Garlic powder
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

Burger Prep

  1. Tear off a burger size chunk of beef, use spatula to SMASH burger. (DO NOT ROLL BEEF AROUND IN YOUR HANDS THE OLD WAY
  2. Once you have all smashed out your beef into flat patties, mix together equal parts Dales and teriyaki sauce, baste onto top of burger
  3. Sprinkle with salt, pepper, and garlic powder

Grilling

  1. Light grill and set it to HI
  2. Once the grill is hot, throw your patties on with the marinaded side DOWN!
  3. Marinde and season the top of the burgers just like before
  4. Cook approx 5 mins and flip
  5. Cook for another 5 mins and check doneness
