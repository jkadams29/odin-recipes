Pan Grilled Chicken

Juicy Pan Grilled Chicken!

A good grilled chicken recipe is something everyone should know how to make. Paired with a healthy side, this is one of the most nutritious meals you can eat!

Check out the recipe below to become and create the chicken that your friends and family will beg for!

Ingredients

  • Boneless chicken breast or thighs
  • Dale's liquid seasoning
  • Teriyaki sauce
  • Garlic powder
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Tony Chacheres seasoning
  • Olive oil
  • Cast iron skillet (or any pan, we won't judge!)

Steps

Preparation

  1. Mix together equal parts Dale's and teriyaki sauce
  2. Put chicken in large bowl or ziplok bag and pour marinade over the chicken

Cooking

  1. Add olive oil to hot skillet, just enough to coat the pan
  2. Add chicken to pan, use basting brush to brush on marinade
  3. Sprinkle with salt, pepper, garlic powder, and Tony's
  4. Allow to cook for about 10 mins, check bottom of chicken for blackening
  5. Flip chicken, repeat basting and seasoning
