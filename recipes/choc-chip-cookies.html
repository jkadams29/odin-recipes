Chocolate Chip Cookies

Awesome chocolate chip cookies!

Remember those cookies that mom used to make for Christmas? Well, look no further! This is the best chocolate chip cookie recipe you will ever need!

Below you will find a list of every ingredient needed to create these cookies and the instructions to bring them to life!

Ingredients

  • Brown sugar
  • Butter, softened
  • Flour
  • Baking powder
  • Salt
  • Eggs
  • Baking Soda
  • Semi-sweet chocolate chips

Steps

Mixing

  1. Mix together butter, sugar, brown sugar, and eggs in a bowl
  2. Mix together flour, salt, baking powder and baking soda in a separate bowl
  3. Mix dry ingredients into wet mixture until dough is formed
  4. Add in chocolate chips

Baking

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Bake cookies for 10-12 mins, depending on preferred texture
